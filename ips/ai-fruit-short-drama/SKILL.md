# AI Fruit Short Drama

Use this as a FinalRound-specific short-drama style library. The fruit is the visual shell; the product story still has to be Live Interview Copilot / Desktop Stealth App in a real interview situation.

Do not let the output become a children's cartoon or random fruit comedy. Every beat must map to job-search pressure, interview freezing, rambling, or real-time answer structure.

## Core Logic

AI fruit drama works because a simple human anxiety becomes physically visible through an absurd fruit character.

For FinalRound:

```
fruit candidate enters interview pressure
→ body exaggerates the emotion
→ interviewer/screen stays calm and silent
→ fruit nearly collapses or rambles
→ FinalRound gives structure
→ fruit regains shape, posture, or voice
```

## Workflow

### Step 0: Choose the Fruit Metaphor

Pick fruit based on the user's pain point:

- banana: slipping, panic, unstable confidence
- apple: polished outside, blank inside
- lemon: sour feedback, harsh interviewer
- grape cluster: too many interview rounds or panel pressure
- avocado: "not ripe yet" seniority signal
- strawberry: cute but overwhelmed, high emotional expressiveness
- orange: segmented answer structure, turning chaos into sections

### Step 1: Match a Scene Structure

Open `refs/场景结构索引表.md` and choose 2-3 structures if the user is still deciding.

Short direction format:

```
产品面：Live Interview Copilot / Desktop Stealth App
ICP心理：[一句话]
风格模块：AI Fruit Short Drama
水果隐喻：[水果 + 对应痛点]
场景结构：[索引编号 + 名称]
3秒hook：[第一个荒诞画面或英文台词]
FinalRound转折：[第几秒进入产品edge]
禁止项：不儿童动画化，不脱离面试，不出现字幕、水印、可读UI、logo、中文对白
```

### Step 2: Write the 15s Prompt

Use one 15-second vertical 9:16 prompt. Keep the fruit character expressive and physical, but keep the interview setup legible.

Required elements:

- Anthropomorphic fruit character with clear eyes, mouth, expression, and body movement.
- Real laptop/desktop interview setup, even if the candidate is fruit.
- Silent interviewer or pressure source on the computer screen.
- FinalRound enters as a short spoken action or visible non-readable structure cue.
- All spoken dialogue must be English.

## Visual Grammar

Use:

- bright, colorful, absurd social-video look
- fruit face close-ups
- exaggerated sweating, rolling, wobbling, peeling, splitting, or reassembling
- clear laptop interview setup
- simple physical metaphor
- short-drama reactions and hard cuts

Avoid:

- children's animation tone
- fantasy world with no computer interview
- too many fruit characters in 15 seconds
- readable UI, subtitles, logo, title card, end card
- gore, rotting, or body horror
- Chinese spoken dialogue

## Dialogue Rules

Fruit dialogue should be short and direct.

Good shapes:

- "I know this answer."
- "Why am I peeling?"
- "My brain just rolled away."
- "I need structure."
- "Open FinalRound AI."

The interviewer usually stays silent. The screen and silence should create pressure.

## QA

Before finalizing:

- Does the fruit metaphor match the interview pain point?
- Is the fruit still in a real computer interview?
- Does FinalRound solve structure, not magically guarantee an offer?
- Is the prompt visually simple enough for 15 seconds?
- Did it avoid children's cartoon tone, readable UI, logo, subtitles, watermarks, and Chinese spoken dialogue?
